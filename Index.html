Stray Kids, a South Korean boy group formed under JYP Entertainment, made their debut in 2018.
Comprising eight members - Bangchan, Lee Know, Changbin, Han, Hyunjin, Seungmin, Felix, and I.N. - their rapid ascent to fame can be attributed to their unique musical style.
Their distinctive approach, which blends various genres and rhythmic patterns, aligns with the emerging trend of noise music within the K-pop scene. Notably, their discography is a testament to their versatility, constantly exploring a broad spectrum of genres and styles. This showcases not only their penchant for experimentation but also their innovative spirit.
